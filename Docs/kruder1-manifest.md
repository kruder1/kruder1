# KRUDER1  
## Brand Manifesto

---

Kruder1 isn’t for everyone.  
And it was never meant to be.

We don’t believe in competing on price.  
We don’t believe in “cheaper”.  
We believe in offering something that’s noticed, remembered,  
and worth paying for.

The photo booth isn’t dead.  
It’s saturated with the same experience.

Kruder1 exists for photo booth owners who understand  
that differentiation isn’t about doing more —  
it’s about offering something others can’t.

We don’t promise clients.  
That part is on you.

What we do offer is a tool  
that changes the conversation,  
raises perceived value,  
and helps you close better events.

You don’t sell photos.  
You sell differentiation.

One credit. One image.  
No subscriptions. No expiration. No fine print.

Built by people with over 14 years of real-world experience  
running photo booths at real events.

We’re not chasing volume.  
We’re focused on owners who know how to sell value.

If you’re tired of the price war,  
you’re in the right place.

If you’re not,  
Kruder1 probably isn’t for you.
