**Scene & Atmosphere:** Nighttime rooftop setting with heavy rain falling dramatically. The background features a glowing city skyline with vibrant neon bokeh in deep blues, intense reds, and vivid yellows. Wet surfaces reflect city lights.

**Lighting & Tech:** Cinematic cool key light (5600K) highlighting raindrops on the skin and the wet texture of the suit. Warm city reflections bounce off the suit. Shot on a 50mm lens at f/2.0. Ultra-sharp focus on the face and water droplets, shallow depth of field, 8K resolution, photorealistic, high dynamic range. No watermarks or AI artifacts.
